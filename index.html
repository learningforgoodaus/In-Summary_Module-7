In Summary: Wellbeing & Worker Safety

Key points from Module 7: Maintaining resilience and monitoring professional stress.

Monitoring Stress Levels

  • Utilise the Stress Continuum to identify early indicators of vicarious trauma or emotional fatigue.

  • Recognise that shifting from 'Healthy' to 'Reacting' is a cue to implement self-care routines immediately.

Proactive Self-Care

  • Implement transition routines at the end of each day to physically and mentally separate work from personal life.

  • Practise personal check-ins after heavy interactions to ground yourself and acknowledge emotional impact.

Seeking Support

  • Utilise your organisation's Employee Assistance Program (EAP) for confidential, professional guidance during challenging times.

  • Acknowledge that reaching out for help is a sign of strength and a vital part of staying healthy while you support others.
